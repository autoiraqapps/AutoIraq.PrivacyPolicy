Privacy Policy

Welcome to AutoIraq, a platform designed to empower individuals to list, discover, and purchase vehicles in a streamlined and user-friendly manner. This Privacy Policy describes how AutoIraq Technologies (“AutoIraq,” “we,” “our,” or “us”) collects, uses, discloses, stores, and safeguards your personal information in connection with your use of our mobile application, website, and related services (collectively, the “Services”). This Policy applies to all users, including buyers, sellers, and visitors. By accessing or using the Services, you consent to the data practices described in this Policy. If you do not agree, please discontinue use immediately.

1. Information We Collect

We may collect the following categories of personal and non-personal information including information you provide directly such as registration data when creating an account, which may include your full name, email address, phone number, password, and an optional profile picture. This also includes data you input when listing a vehicle for sale, such as make, model, year, price, location, photos, and any description you provide. Additionally, we may collect the content of messages sent between users, call metadata including duration and timestamps, as well as feedback, reviews, and support requests. While AutoIraq does not currently process payments within the app, in the future we may allow secure payment options and collect billing and transactional information.

1.1 Information Collected Automatically

We collect device data such as device type, manufacturer, operating system, and unique device identifiers like IMEI or MAC address. We also gather usage data including pages or screens viewed, time spent on the app, interactions with features, crash logs, search queries, and referral sources. With your consent, we collect location data such as GPS or approximate location to help show local listings and optimize app features. Additionally, we use cookies, beacons, and various analytics tools to improve usability and personalize the content you see.

1.2 Information From Third Parties

If you sign in through Google, Apple, or Facebook, we may receive basic profile information and your email address. We may also receive demographic or interest data from advertising partners to personalize your ad experiences. Additionally, certain technical information may be shared with our service providers and vendors to support security, analytics, and hosting of our platform.
